# CookieTmw
- I got the idea of making this website from the previous project i made. I know in foreign we have these fortune cookies, but not in India. Throughout the whole covide i used to watch just unboxing mukbang and different videos, one of which was fortune cookies related. Since we don't have these cookies i now finally fulfilled a wish (kind of ig) of mine to open fortune cookies. 
# Fearures
- The color theme of the whole website is common the shade of pink which is like rose gold but without gold and some peach.
- No background image has been used
- On landing page there is a floating svg of a hamster chef.
- Shop.html is for the cookies.
- Only 3 cookies can be claimed in a day
- on clicking claim one will get a confirmation pop up.
- reveal.html displays a floatng cookie clicking it displays a message.
- collection.html is the collection of all the messages.
- In all websites pink snowing sparkles are used.
- All svgs except the landing page one is of butterfly.

# How to Use
- After you land on the first page, click on "shop".
- In shop claim every cookie one by one.
- To claim it click on claim and confirm.
- Click the floating cookie until the message is revealed
- Click on save button to save the message in your collection.
- To see all of your messages claimed, click on collected button.
- That's it
 
 # Languages used
-The languages used are: -Html(basic) -CSS(styling) -Java(for working of the site)
